About TechNival 2018

TechNival 2018 is going to be the country’s biggest tech carnival for the enthusiatic minds of Bangladesh, organized by the dept. of ECE, Khulna University of Engineering & Technology. We are here with segments for University, College and School students. TechNival 2018 is in its endeavor to provide a platform for the youth to showcase their talents and skills through competitions, displaying cutting edge technology.Our goal is to encourage innovations toward humanity. We believe this competiton will initiate a collaborative environment for the robotic minds of the country and introduce them with new technologies in robotics.Besides, this year we have come up with some segments for the school and college students to show their latent talents.
